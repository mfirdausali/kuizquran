# Kuiz Quran — The Whole Journey, in ASCII

_From the first tap to lifelong retention — and the flywheel it feeds._
_Timings are grounded in `srs-engine.json` (QuranFSRS-Implicit, R=0.92)._

There are **five zoom levels**. Each is the same journey seen from further back:

```
   ZOOM 1   One WORD's moment ......... seconds        "the atom"
   ZOOM 2   One WORD's lifetime ....... ~130 days      "acquisition → welded"
   ZOOM 3   One SURAH's arc ........... weeks–months   "the movie, act by act"
   ZOOM 4   One LEARNER's year ........ 6–18 months     "beginner → carrier"
   ZOOM 5   The DATA flywheel ......... years           "the moat / the product"
```

## ZOOM 2 — One word's lifetime (~130 days to "welded")

The same word, met once, then scheduled to return **exactly as it's about to be forgotten**.
The gap widens each time it survives. Below: the real FSRS progression for one word.

```
  STATE:   NEW → LEARNING → REVIEW(young) ─────────→ REVIEW(mature) → WELDED
                                                                        │
  memory   full ●                                                       │ survives inside
  strength      │●.                        pulse = a scheduled recall   │ whole-ayah &
   ▲            │  '●.                      ▼   ▼      ▼         ▼        │ next-ayah recall
   │            │     '●.___(review)___●.__/ ●./ __●./ _______●./       │
   │  forget ‑ ‑│‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ ‑ line
   │  line      │        (each review resets strength higher = longer next gap)
   └────────────┴───────┬──────┬───────────┬──────────────┬────────────────► time
                DAY 0    DAY 1  DAY 7      DAY 18         DAY 55        DAY 130
                intro    R1     R2          R3             R4            welded
                S≈3d    S→7d   S→18d       S→55d          S→130d        long-term
                Good    Good   Good        Easy           Good          ✓

  interval widens:  0d ──1d──► ──6d──► ────11d────► ───────37d──────► ────75d────►

  ┌─ IF A LAPSE (taps wrong at R2) ───────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │   S≈7d ══crash══► S≈2-3d   (NOT reset to zero — "sabr jamīl")               │
  │   Difficulty +1  → ALL this word's future gaps shrink automatically         │
  │   → short relearning step (~10 min, then ~2d) → rejoins the climb           │
  │   repeated lapses → LEECH → routed to a scaffolded mini-lesson, not looping  │
  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  TOTAL TOUCHES to weld one word:  ~5–7 recalls over ~4 months.
  TOTAL TIME SPENT on that word:   under 1 minute, spread across 4 months.
```

The magic: **effort is tiny, spacing does the work.** You "own" the word for months
after under a minute of total contact — because the reviews landed at the right moments.

## ZOOM 3 — One surah's arc (Surah Yusuf: weeks → months)

Words don't live alone — they hang off the **19-act movie** (or the tighter **12-point ring**).
Acts unlock in order; you rebuild the story scene by scene. The ring means recalling one half
cues its mirror.

```
   THE 12-POINT RING (chiasmus)         each act = a cluster of word-cards
   descent ↓                    ↑ ascent   that unlock & weld together
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  1  Yusuf's dream ●───────────────────────────● 12 dream fulfilled│
   │  2  brothers plot  ●─────────────────────────● 11 brothers learn  │
   │  3  wife seduces    ●───────────────────────● 10 wife confesses   │
   │  4  ladies convince  ●─────────────────────● 9 ladies confess     │
   │  5  goes to jail      ●───────────────────● 8 released            │
   │  6  king's dream       ●─────────────────● 7 dream interpreted    │
   │                         └──── PIVOT ────┘                          │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
        recalling one side cues its mirror ↔  (the ring IS a mnemonic)

   UNLOCK GATE (why it can't be rushed):
     Act N+1 unlocks only when Act N's words reach REVIEW(young) stability.
     → you literally cannot skip ahead → the surah is memorized end-to-end, in order.

   PROGRESS OVER TIME  (Surah Yusuf = 111 ayahs · 1,777 words)
     week 1   ▓▓░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░  Acts 1–2  (dream) opened, first words welding
     week 3   ▓▓▓▓▓░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░  Acts 1–5  (into the pit)
     week 6   ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓░░░░░░░░░░░░  Acts 1–9  (the torn shirt)
     week 10  ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓░░░░░░░░  Acts 1–13 (rise to power)
     week 16  ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓░░░  Acts 1–18 (the prostration)
     ~5-6 mo  ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓  all 19 acts opened; welding continues in background

   NB: "opened" ≠ "welded." New acts open in months; the last words finish welding
       a few months after that — review load then drops to near-zero maintenance.
```
